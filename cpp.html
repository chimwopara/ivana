Child Protection Policy

Ivana Foundation has zero tolerance for child abuse. We are committed to the safety, rights, and well-being of every child we serve.

1. Our Commitment

We believe that every child is created in the image of God and deserves to grow up in a safe, nurturing environment free from exploitation, abuse, and neglect. This policy applies to all staff, volunteers, board members, and partners of the Ivana Foundation.

2. Core Principles

  • Best Interest of the Child: The welfare of the child is our paramount consideration in all decisions and activities.
  • Zero Tolerance: We will not tolerate any form of abuse—physical, sexual, emotional, or neglect.
  • Accountability: We hold all personnel and partners accountable to strict behavioral standards.

3. Media & Photography Standards

To protect the dignity and safety of children, we adhere to strict guidelines regarding the collection and use of images:

  • We will never publish full names combined with exact locations of vulnerable children.
  • Images will act to preserve dignity and will not portray children as helpless victims but as individuals with potential.
  • All media collection requires informed consent from guardians.

4. Reporting Procedures

Any suspicion or allegation of abuse must be reported immediately. Ivana Foundation maintains a mandatory reporting mechanism. If you suspect a breach of this policy, please contact our Safeguarding Officer immediately at safeguarding@ivanafoundation.org.
