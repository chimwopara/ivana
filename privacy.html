Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

At Ivana Foundation, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information when you visit our website, make a donation, sign up for a newsletter, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number, address).
  • Donation history and payment preferences (note: we do not store full credit card numbers; these are processed securely by our payment partners like Fillout/Stripe).
  • Communication preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process donations and issue tax receipts.
  • Communicate with you about the impact of your gifts and upcoming needs.
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and financial reporting standards.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Our website uses SSL encryption, and all financial transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant gateways.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to turn off all cookies via your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us at:

contact@ivanafoundation.org
